   declares extraOutputs=[build/reproos-installed.qcow2] but the
   engine's promotion stage didn't surface it under .repro/output/.
   Artifact IS produced at the build/ path; the engine cache HIT
   reports "executed" but skips the copy.  Likely a recipe DSL
   shape gap (no actionId-shaped output decl); needs M9.R.50.4.1
   follow-up.  Does NOT block M9.R.50.5 boot smoke -- the boot
   driver reads from the build/ path directly.

2. install-root's copyLiveKernelAndInitrd patched to look at
   <source>/boot/vmlinuz first (M9.R.50.3.5).  The previous
   live-medium hardcoded paths are still present as fallbacks for
   the live-ISO autorun path (which remains supported per the
   spec's Section 5 "what's kept").

3. The seeded /boot/initrd.img is the iso recipe's live-boot
   initramfs.  On the installed disk it will probe for
   /live/filesystem.squashfs, not find one, and drop to a busybox
   rescue shell.  The boot smoke (M9.R.50.5) will use this rescue
   shell to verify the rootfs is mountable + the DE binaries are
   present; full systemd boot to multi-user.target requires a
   real "boot-from-disk" initramfs and is documented as a remaining
   gap in m9r50_complete.txt.
