The HONEST closure cost for Gap 6's residual items is 12+ new recipes
(shadow-utils, sudo, glibc-locale-data, IANA tzdata, console-tools,
parted, e2fsprogs, dosfstools, gdisk, cryptsetup, lvm2, btrfs-progs,
grub2 + grub2 modules). Each is a 1-2hr authoring slice; collectively
they exceed the M9.R.26 wall-time budget. The honest move is to
mark them M9.R.27 candidates with a per-item ETA in the existing
TODO(M9.R.27) markers in build-base-rootfs.sh.

Note: build-base-rootfs.sh's kept-in-apt list is NOT a from-source-
coverage gap — it is a HOSTED-INIT-CHAIN gap. The from-source systemd
+ util-linux + kmod recipes exist but are not yet wired through the
ISO's PID-1 boot chain (initramfs init script + DESTDIR-rooted /usr/
layout). Switching the live ISO from the apt-installed systemd-sysv to
the from-source systemd is a separate architecturally-large milestone.
