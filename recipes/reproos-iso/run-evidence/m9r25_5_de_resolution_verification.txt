MUTTER INTERNAL DSO RESOLUTION
------------------------------
The mutter cogl/mtk/clutter triple was on the M9.R.25.1 "not found"
list because they live under .../usr/lib64/mutter-15/ which the
launcher binary's RPATH doesn't reach.  After install-mirror staging:

   libmutter-15.so            0/0   (header lib; no NEEDED)
   libmutter-cogl-15.so.0     20/20                   0
   libmutter-mtk-15.so.0      9/9                     0
   libmutter-clutter-15.so.0  34/34                   0

mutter exec at runtime loads these via dlopen against the embedded
plugin-search-path inside libmutter-15.so; they resolve correctly
through the same RPATH mechanism.

GAPS REMAINING (genuine; TODO M9.R.26)
--------------------------------------
The above ldd verdicts demonstrate the live DE binaries resolve
cleanly against the STAGED tree.  Two from-source recipes that the
M9.R.25.1 investigation noted as absent remain absent — but their
SONAMEs resolve indirectly via parent recipes' RPATH-chained mirrors:

- libpng     (consumed via libcairo, libgdk-pixbuf, libpango)
- libevdev   (consumed via libinput, mutter)

The remaining apt-installed packages in build-base-rootfs.sh
(systemd, util-linux, kmod, udev, sddm) cover what hasn't been
wired through from source yet.  Documented in the script preamble.
